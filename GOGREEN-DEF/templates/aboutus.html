About us

Hello user, we are six management engineers students at Politecnico di Torino. We created GreenGo for several reasons, in this little paragraph we will try to explain you the main ones. Firstly, the sustainability theme is becoming more and more a center one in our everyday life. In particular Turin is in the top three most polluted cities of Italy, a primate not so good for both the attractiveness of the city itself and for its citizens health. So why do not accelerate this transaction to sustainable means of transport? At the same time we are students, someone offsite, someone not, and we have needs regarding transportation infrastructure and we usually do not have so much money or so much time. We try to explain better: if you need a sharing company mean of transport, you just look at the app corresponding to that specific sharing company, so you do not have any idea if there are other sharing companies equally near to you available at a lower price, or more comfortable to your specific need in that moment. Or better, you can have this information by visiting other sharing companies websites and losing that time just mentioned up. So due to these needs, we created GreenGo, the green app of means of transports which allows you to choose the cheaper mean or the one it is more addressable to you in that moment. All surrounded by the central theme of sustainability, allowing only green sharing companies to collaborate with us and incentivating users to reserve green means in order to increase their score and earn fantastic coupons offered by environmental agencies and sponsor. This is what GreenGo does and can help for.
